Welcome to alpha.gov.uk

Alpha.gov.uk is an experimental prototype of a single UK Government website. It aims to be as simple as possible, and to place the needs and expectations of citizens first. Developed in under three months by a small, multi-disciplinary team within the Government Digital Service, it is a direct response to Martha Lane Fox's recent review of gov.uk, which demanded revolution, not evolution, in the way Government services are delivered online.

The prototype is not complete and may not be accurate. Please don't rely on it.

But please do tell us what you think of it. Which aspects work well? Which don't? What works? What doesn't? The point of putting this incomplete prototype public is to stress test what are relatively radical design ideas before a huge amount of time and public money has been spent. We need your feedback!
